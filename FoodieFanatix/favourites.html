Favorites Gallery

Welcome to our curated list of favorite restaurants, where we share the culinary gems that have captured our hearts and palates. From hidden local treasures to world-renowned dining establishments, these spots offer unforgettable experiences and mouthwatering dishes. Whether you're a foodie looking for your next gastronomic adventure or simply in search of a great meal, our favorites list promises to guide you to some of the best dining experiences around. Bon appétit!

RH Rooftop Restaurant - New York

RH is named for the fancy furniture store it’s on top of, and if you’re unfamiliar with Restoration Hardware, it’s worth coming here just to walk around and check out couches that cost as much as cars. The menu isn’t super exciting, but they deliver a perfect burrata with grilled tomatoes and a great ribeye sandwich. There’s no hard liquor here, which should be your first indication this isn’t a typical rooftop party restaurant. (Your second indication is that RH closes at 9pm.) Still, this is a good option for fun group dinners when you want to get dressed up and experience amazing views.

  • Open: Mon-Sun 10:00 - 21:00
  • Closed: None
  • Average price: Dinner $52 / Lunch $24,00
  • Address: 9 9th Ave, New York, NY 10014, United States

Flower Wall Mone - Tokyo

A restaurant with beautiful, giant wall art that welcomes guests. The varied cuisine and night parfaits, based on the theme of flowers, are incredibly appealing. They have a lineup of true masterpieces carefully made by the chef, including their signature dish the MONE Bouquet Salad, the Black Tea and Apricot Parfait, arranged with lovely flowers and lace, and the 2-colored Japanese Style Mushroom Hotpot, made with authentic Chinese chili peppers. The restaurant interior makes it a truly extraordinary space.

  • Open: Mon-Fri 13:00 - 23:30 / Saturday, Sunday 11:30 - 23:30
  • Closed: None
  • Average price: Dinner 4,000 JPY / Lunch 2,000 JPY
  • Address: B1F, Takayama Land Hall, 3-5-3, Shinjuku, Shinjuku-ku, Tokyo

Girafe -
Paris

Nestled in the Cité de l’Architecture at the Trocadero, this 16th arrondissement gem boasts an interior inspired by 1930s Art Deco, featuring cream-colored banquettes and black and white marble countertops. For the full Girafe experience, secure a table on the terrace. As a premier seafood restaurant, starting with oysters is a must. The sharing platters are also a highlight, with options like the tasting platter, which includes six oysters, shrimps, Norway lobster, and whelks, and the seafood platter, crowned with half a blue lobster. Girafe offers a glamorous dining experience that perfectly blends exquisite seafood with stunning views.

  • Open: Mon-Sun 17h00 - 23h30
  • Closed: None
  • Average price: Dinner €100
  • 1 place du Trocadéro et du 11 Novembre

Le Bernardin -
New York

Le Bernardin is renowned for making its guests feel extraordinary. The service exceeds expectations: swap plates with a friend, and a server seamlessly appears to assist, ensuring you have the correct utensils for your new dish. The expansive rectangular dining room is elegantly lit with soft spotlights that perfectly illuminate your plate. The atmosphere is serene, with gentle jazz playing and conversations kept to a hushed tone. Many servers sport French accents, whether authentic or perfected for the role, adding to the sophisticated ambiance. The sommeliers, often women, are approachable and knowledgeable. Le Bernardin truly lives up to its stellar reputation, offering an indulgent dining experience where luxury is measured not just by price, but by the exceptional quality and service.

  • Open: Mon-Sun 17h00 - 23h30
  • Closed: None
  • Average price: Dinner $190
  • Address: Le Bernardin 787 Seventh Avenue New York

Ithaa Undersea Restaurant -
Maldives

Ithaa Undersea Restaurant offers a unique dining experience five meters below the ocean’s surface. With stunning panoramic views of coral gardens, this underwater setting creates an enchanting ambiance with a 270-degree view of the ocean that feels both intimate and expansive. The fusion menu, blending Western and Maldivian flavors, is complemented by an expertly curated wine list. Impeccable service and the mesmerizing underwater vista make dining at Ithaa a memorable event. Perfect for special occasions, Ithaa promises an unforgettable culinary journey beneath the waves. It’s more than just a meal; it’s an immersive experience that engages all the senses, from the exceptional fusion cuisine to the captivating underwater scenery.

  • Open: Mon-Sun 17h00 - 23h30
  • Closed: None
  • Average price: Dinner $240
  • Address: Rangali Island, 20077, Maldives

Vampire Cafe -
Tokyo

Step into the [Vampire House], a cafe and restaurant designed to transport you to Medieval Europe. The ambiance is spellbinding, with dazzling chandeliers, red velour curtains, and dim lighting casting a haunting glow over an altar, coffin, and cross. Skeleton figurines and a grimoire-like menu enhance the eerie atmosphere. The menu features unique French and Italian-inspired dishes with intriguing names, and the colorful, fascinating cocktails are a hit with patrons. Staff in vampire costumes add to the immersive experience, making dining here both playful and memorable. Many visitors return, captivated by the gothic charm and the enigmatic manager, the [Earl of Rose]. For a night of gothic elegance and culinary delights, the Vampire House offers an unforgettable experience.

  • Open: Mon-Sun 17h00 - 23h30
  • Closed: None
  • Average price: Dinner 3,000 JPY
  • Address: 7F, La Paix Bldg., 6-7-6 Ginza, Chuo-ku, Tokyo
